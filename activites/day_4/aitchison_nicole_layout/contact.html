Frequently Asked Question

I’ve heard there’s some REALLY beautiful women and men that work at Fat Wreck Chords. How do I contact the label?

Make donations to the Beer Fund.

For general inquiries, email mailbag@fatwreck.com

For webstore inquiries, email mailorder@fatwreck.com and include your full name and order number from your confirmation email.

Aside from that, you can typically find us at any one of the bars or pubs in the San Francisco Bay Area. With the obvious exception of Hemlock Tavern, Zeitgeist, Moby Dick’s, Kezar Pub, Mad Dog In The Fog, Yancy’s, and Lucky 13. Duh.

Do you guys take demos? How does our band get on Fat?

Oh we get demos. Shitloads of them. Mike thinks it would be lame not to accept demos, but then again, he doesn’t have to listen to the fuckin’ things. If you must send us a demo, here is some advice: work on your songs, practice, and play shows. It takes years of hard work to be a band that people care about, and once you’re there, labels will know about you. Another thing, talent is not a requirement for Fat bands, but partying skills are.
